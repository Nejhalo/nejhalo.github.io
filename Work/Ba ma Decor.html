Introduction

Overview

My Role: as a product designer

BamaDecor is a social platform designed to connect professionals in the decoration industry (such as cabinet makers, carpenters, interior designers, and other specialists) with potential clients. The platform caters not only to those working with wooden artifacts but broadly to the entire decoration and construction industry. BamaDecor serves as a localized, industry-specific adaptation of platforms like Dribbble, tailored to the Iranian market's unique needs.
