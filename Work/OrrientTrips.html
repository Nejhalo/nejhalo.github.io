OrrientTrips Case study

.

TLDR:

Orient Trips is a modern platform for travel planning, aiming to simplify the travel experience for international visitors to Iran. Users can explore destinations, create personalized travel itineraries, and book all travel services in one place. The platform represents simplicity, rich cultural experiences, and hassle-free travel.




2. Problem Statement

International travelers visiting Iran often face the following challenges:

  • Scattered and unreliable information about destinations
  • Difficulty in planning and coordinating schedules, hotels, and activities
  • Lack of consistency in UI components and design
  • Concerns about incorrect bookings or unreliable services

Orient Trips' goal: To solve these problems by providing a seamless, user-centered experience.

Solution

Orient Trips offers a comprehensive platform that simplifies the travel experience from start to finish:

  • Discover Destinations: Presenting travel destinations with descriptions, photos, and essential tips
  • Ready & Personalized Itineraries: Users can choose pre-made plans or create itineraries tailored to their preferences
  • Travel Booking & Services: Booking hotels, tours, activities, transportation, travel insurance, visa support, and 24/7 customer assistance

5. UX Highlights

  • Simple and intuitive user interface with easy access to information
  • Comprehensive display of each destination and activity
  • Quick, secure, and reliable booking process

6. Outcome & Value

  • Integrated component system and implementation of Semantic Tokens for colors and typography compatible with Vue.js framework
  • Enhanced brand UI Kit and implementation of Material Design System to reduce CSS bloat
  • Improved user experience through UX Writing and intuitive UI design
  • Close collaboration with development and product teams to align design with business goals
  • Increased conversion rate and user engagement by optimizing key booking paths and focusing on user pain points
  • Designing content and UX messages for error scenarios, empty states, and edge cases to create a more human-centered experience
