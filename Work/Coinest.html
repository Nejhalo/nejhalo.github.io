Coinest Case study

This is a project for the Amanj Academy boot camp.

TLDR:

This Android application serves as a platform for personal financial management. It allows you to create a budget for your monthly income, keep track of your expenses, and write down your costs every month. The most significant feature of the app is that it enables you to compile a list of your dreams (goals) and determine the cost of achieving them. You can also use the app's wallet as a safe place to save money towards achieving your goals. Another feature of the application is the ability to transfer money. However, you must purchase the premium version to access all the features. And transfer money is another feature of this app but user for fully use all the features, They must buy the premium version.

How did it start?

This project is for Amanj Academy, which is a boot camp organized by Webinar Academy. We were given the challenge of creating an Android app within a time frame of about three weeks.

Given the challenging conditions in Iran and the financial difficulties faced by people, especially employees who struggle with saving, but require more accurate management of their expenses, I decided to design this product. By creating new and improved experiences compared to the current conditions, with the help of a digital product designed for the Android platform, it will assist them in achieving financial goals and monthly savings. Besides, it will help maintain control, increase awareness of expenses, and transform their financial habits.

Our target audience is the working population living independently, and we have decided to focus our product on them.
